The University’s Quality Enhancement Plan (QEP) integrates the NACE Career Competency Framework into the University’s educational programs, including its General Education program, to better prepare its students for success in the ever-changing job market.

Adoption of the NACE Career Competency framework demonstrates the University's commitment to producing career-ready graduates and will strengthen partnerships with employers.

What is Midway University's QEP?

Our QEP theme is titled R.A.C.E. From Starting Gate to Finish Line: Readiness Accelerates Career Excellence.

Why does this matter?

The goal is to boost your confidence, make you career-ready, and increase opportunities for internships, jobs, and grad school.
Think of your time at Midway like a horse race—from the starting gate to the finish line, we’re helping you prepare to cross into your future career successfully!
